Skin Types

Oily

Oily skin, also known as seborrhea, is a common skin condition that occurs when the skin produces too much sebum, an oily substance that can cause acne too.

Normal

Normal skin is a well-balanced skin type that is neither too dry nor too oily. It has a regular texture, a clean, soft appearance, and no imperfections.

Dry

Dry skin is a common condition that occurs when the skin loses too much water and oil. It can cause the skin to feel rough, itchy, flaky, or scaly, and it can appear red or ashy gray. Other symptoms include: Fine cracks, Peeling, Pain, stinging, or burning, Wrinkled texture, and Skin infections.
